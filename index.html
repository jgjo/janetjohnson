Janet Johnson

I'm a mixed-methods HCI Researcher pursuing a PhD at UC San Diego. My research mainly focuses on eXtended/Mixed Reality mediated collaboration.

I'm currently advised by Dr. Nadir Weibel and am associated with both the HXI Lab and the Design Lab. I am also associated with the XR Safety Initiative where I serve as an advisor for Medical XR research. In the past, I've worked with Reality Labs, PwC, Microsoft, and Juniper Networks.

I will be graduating in Winter 2023, and would love to chat about full-time research opportunities if you have any! Reach out to me at janetjohnson@ucsd.edu or on LinkedIn.
